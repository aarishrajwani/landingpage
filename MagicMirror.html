What are Magic Mirrors/Smart Mirrors?

Magic Mirror is an open source smart mirror platform that allows you make your mirror into your own personal assistant. On the surface these smart mirrors look similar to a standard mirror, but what makes these mirrors unique is the information it displays on the mirror. On the face of the mirror is text and graphics that provides the user with basic information like the time, date and calendar. The mirror also has the ability to house more complex features such as google assistant, dad jokes, gmail feed, weather forecast and much more.

How do these Smart Mirrors work?

The heart of the smart mirror tends to be some sort of a display, ranging from an old tablet to a monitor and even a tv. These displays allow the mirror to display the necessary information on the surface of the mirror. The next key element is the mirror itself. A smart mirror requires a special type of mirror called a 2-way mirror. These mirrors allow light to enter from one side of the mirror and reflect light on the other side of the mirror. This allows the text to be visible while also allowing the mirror to be reflective. The next major component is the brains of the operation. In DIY projects similar to this, the software is run off of a microcomputer known as a Raspberry Pi. These small but mighty computers allow the display to know exactly what to show.

A Look Into My Smart Mirror Project

A bored summer day in the midst of a pandemic led to one of my favorite projects I have done. After almost a year of learning, researching, downloading, modifying, writing, building and perfecting, I had finally achieved the final product I desired. A well crafted smart mirror that doubles as my information board and personal assistant.

Equipped in my mirror are modules that show the current time, calendar feed from my iCloud and google calendars, live gmail feed, the current weather and weather forecast, compliments to boost my ego, and dad jokes to give me quick laugh. Since I am a little bit extra, I decided to also include a google assistant that gives me detailed responses to questions, shows my photos from google photos, display images from google, play videos from youtube, and even play my favorite songs from spotify so I can jam out 24/7!
